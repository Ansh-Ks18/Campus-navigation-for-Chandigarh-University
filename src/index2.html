Some Reviews About Chandigarh University Campus

Only for learning purpose

Infrastructure of the university is as beautiful. In every block there is library available which is perk for student for self study. In central library there are many books available. PC is available in labs which up to date updated. Campus size is approx. 250 acre. Every class has approx. 70 student in each.Campus life is best part of this university here you will get the students from all over the India as well as international so you will get a lot of exposure. People here are so friendly. There is place called Fountain where you can sit in free time with your friends. There are two grounds available. There is so many clubs available approx. for every activity, dance, singing, theatre, games coding, etc. There is every month event or fest is happening in fest they call singers, DJ, actors, etc.

SATYAM RAJ
Bachelor of Engineering [B.E]

We have various academic blocks in which your respective classes will be held. The structure and architecture of the buildings are quite impressive with raw brick design which gives a premium institute feel to the students, I have seen these types of design similar in some IIM's. Overall you won't be having any problem in your classes.The crowd is a good and a bad point. It is a bad point because the University enrolls many students at a time, but on the brighter side you will get to interact with many students of various cultural background and trust me this is very important for your character development.?
